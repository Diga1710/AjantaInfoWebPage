Information About Cave 23

Cave 23 – An Incomplete Cave with Grand Intentions

Ajanta Cave 23 is an unfinished Mahayana vihara, slightly larger than Cave 22, and located between Caves 22 and 24. Although the excavation was incomplete, the initial layout includes a veranda, pillared hall, and shrine chamber.

Some of the pillars and doorframes show partially carved motifs, including floral designs and rough outlines of guardian figures. The shrine contains a Buddha figure, also in early carving stages. Cave 23 reflects the ambitious plans for spiritual and architectural development that were halted midway, likely due to political changes or resource constraints.
